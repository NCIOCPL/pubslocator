Shipping & Handling

Are there shipping and handling charges?

  • NCI publications are free.
  • 20 items or fewer: No shipping and handling charge.
  • More than 20 items (bulk): Shipping and handling charge is 15 cents per item with a minimum charge of $8.00.
    Total items Calculated shipping & handling
    1 to 20 Free
    21 to 75 $8.00
    76 or more (total items - 20) x $0.15
  • Why do we charge? NCI recovers shipping and handling costs for orders of more than 20 items so we can continue to distribute free publications.

Payment methods

  • You must pay by credit card (VISA, MasterCard, or American Express) to order more than 20 items through this Web site. For alternatives, contact us.

Shipping methods

  • All orders ship within 2 business days.
  • 20 items or fewer: Ships via U.S. Postal Service.
  • More than 20 items (bulk): Ships via FedEx.
  • To choose expedited shipping, contact us. You must pay the actual cost of the expedited shipping in addition to the 15 cents per item charge.

Ordering

Ordering publications

  1. Search or Browse for a product
  2. Click the Order Publication button
  3. A pop-up window appears. In the Quantity box, type the number of copies you need, then click Add to Cart.
    • The Limit displayed is the maximum number of copies you can order.
  4. Click Shopping Cart when you are ready to order. In your Shopping Cart, click Check Out.
  5. Enter your shipping information.
    • If you are ordering more than 20 items, we will also ask for billing information to recover the cost of shipping.
  6. Click Verify Order to review your information.
  7. At this step you can review your entire order before it is submitted. If your order is correct, click Submit Order.
  8. Your order is complete and will ship within 2 business days.

Order limits

  • To ensure availability to all, we may limit the number of copies you can order.
  • To print your own copies, choose a product’s View/Print option. See Self-Printing Options to learn more about options for printing your own copies. Selected materials are available with separate Contents & Covers, for easy reproduction as you need them. Organizations can also purchase more copies through Ride-On. See the Contents & Covers Fact Sheet and Ride-On Printing Fact Sheet (PDF) for details about these programs.
  • Cancer patients, their families and friends, and the general public may order as often as necessary to fulfill their needs.
  • Health professionals and organizations may place bulk orders no more than once a month.

More information about publication distribution policies

  • For more information, see National Cancer Institute Fact Sheet 1.20, Ordering NCI Publications (http://www.cancer.gov/cancertopics/factsheet/NCI/order-publications).

APO/FPO Addresses

You can place your order to an APO/FPO address through this Web site. Follow these instructions to enter your shipping information:

  1. Enter your ZIP Code.
  2. City field: choose APO or FPO.
  3. State field: choose
    • Armed Forces Europe, Middle East, Africa, and Canada
    • Armed Forces Americas, or
    • Armed Forces Pacific.

International Orders

  • The United States includes Puerto Rico, U.S. Virgin Islands, Guam, American Samoa and APO/FPO addresses. Orders to other addresses are considered international orders.
  • You cannot place international orders through this Web site. Instead, contact us following the procedure in National Cancer Institute Fact Sheet 1.20, Ordering NCI Publications (http://www.cancer.gov/cancertopics/factsheet/NCI/order-publications).

Privacy & Security

The Cancer Information Service provides confidential service. The only personal information we collect is the information you provide to us. We use secure server software to ensure your privacy. See NCI Privacy and Security Policy (http://www.cancer.gov/policies/page3) for more information.

How will my information be used?

  • If you send us e-mail, order publications, or complete our user survey, we use the information you give us to respond to your request and to improve our service.
  • If you enter your e-mail address when placing an order, we use your e-mail address only to contact you about your order.

How long will you keep my information?

  • For individuals who order publications, we keep names and addresses for 3 months in order to follow up if necessary. Then we delete your personal information.
  • For organizations who order publications, we keep names and addresses indefinitely to expedite repeat orders.
