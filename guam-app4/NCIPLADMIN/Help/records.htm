Records

The default view of a publication record is a View mode where information cannot be edited.

Adding and editing

To edit a publication record you are viewing, click the Edit button. To add a publication, click Add New Publication in the top navigation menu.

Permissions to modify records

Only Administrator users can Edit all sections of a record.

Data Warehouse Staff level users can add a receiving to the Prod History section of a record.

Spell checking

A spell check menu appears at the top and bottom of record pages.

To spell check an entry, select English or Spanish from the language drop-down menu, then click the "abc" button.
